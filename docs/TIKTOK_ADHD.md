# TikTok-like + ADHD-friendly UI direction

## Visual goals
- **High contrast**: near-black background, near-white text, strong accent colors.
- **TikTok-inspired energy**: neon cyan + hot pink accents, bold headings, crisp cards.
- **ADHD-friendly**: short lines, chunky sections, clear hierarchy, scannable bullets, consistent CTAs, reduced cognitive noise.

## Design tokens
- Background: #07070A
- Text: #F5F7FF
- Muted text: rgba(245,247,255,0.72)
- Border: rgba(245,247,255,0.10)
- Accent pink: #FE2C55
- Accent cyan: #25F4EE
- Accent glow: subtle, not blurry

## Typography
- Display: **Sora** (700–800)
- Body: **Inter** or **Sora** (400–600)
- Max line length: ~60–70ch
- Larger base font on mobile (16–18px)

## ADHD patterns
- Replace long paragraphs with:
  - 1-line claim
  - 2–4 bullet benefits
- Use “Quick summary” cards at top of sections.
- Strong section headings + short subtitles.
- Reduce animation intensity; respect prefers-reduced-motion.
