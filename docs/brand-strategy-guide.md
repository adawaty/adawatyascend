# Brand Strategy Guide

**You don’t need louder marketing. You need a clearer story + a sharper offer.**

This guide is a practical blueprint you can apply immediately to:
- clarify *who* you serve and *why you win*
- tighten messaging so the right buyers self-select
- turn your website into a conversion system (not a brochure)
- create a simple 30‑day execution plan

> **How to use this guide**: start with the *Positioning One‑Pager*, then follow the 30‑day plan at the end.

---

## 1) The positioning equation (simple, usable)

Great positioning is the shortest path to demand. Use this sentence:

> **We help** `[ICP]` **achieve** `[Outcome]` **by** `[Mechanism]` **without** `[Big Pain / Alternative]`.

Examples:
- We help *B2B service firms* *book qualified discovery calls* by *turning expertise into a content + SEO engine* without *hiring a full internal marketing team*.

**Checklist**
- ICP is specific enough to picture a real buyer.
- Outcome is measurable (time saved, revenue, leads, churn, etc.).
- Mechanism is distinct (not “quality,” not “great service”).
- The “without” calls out a real alternative buyers hate.

## 3) Your “why you win” (differentiators that actually matter)

Avoid generic claims like:
- “high quality”
- “great customer service”
- “tailored solutions”

Instead, pick **3 proof-backed differentiators**:

1. **Method** (how you do it)  
2. **Proof** (why it’s safe)  
3. **Speed** (why now)  

### Differentiator format
- **Claim:**
- **Proof:** (case study, metric, demo, before/after)
- **Implication:** why this matters to the buyer

---

## 4) Offer design (make buying feel obvious)

Strong offers reduce decision friction.

### Offer stack
- **Core deliverable:**
- **Fast win (7–14 days):**
- **Risk reversal:** (pilot, milestone-based payment, clear scope)
- **Constraints:** (limited seats, qualification, timeline)

### Pricing sanity checks
- Pricing should match *value*, not hours.
- Fewer packages is better (2–3 max).
- Anchor with outcomes and scope boundaries.

---

## 5) Messaging framework (copy that converts)

Use this structure for the top of your homepage:

1. **Headline:** outcome + differentiation  
2. **Subhead:** for who + how + proof  
3. **CTA:** single primary action  
4. **Credibility bar:** proof points / metrics  
5. **Problem → consequence → solution:** make the pain concrete

---

## 6) Website as a conversion system

A high-converting site is a *sequence*, not sections.

### The conversion sequence
1. **Qualify** (who it’s for / not for)
2. **Educate** (your mechanism)
3. **Prove** (case studies + metrics)
4. **De-risk** (process + guarantees)
5. **Convert** (CTA + frictionless form)

### Must-have pages
- Home
- Services / Solutions
- Pricing (ranges or calculator)
- Work / Case studies
- About (credibility)
- Contact (lead capture)

---

## 7) Search + AI visibility (SEO/AEO fundamentals)

If you want compounding demand, build:
- **1 pillar page** per core service
- **supporting articles** answering buyer questions
- **case studies** with problem → approach → outcome

### Content prompts that rank and convert
- “How to choose `[service]` for `[ICP]`”
- “`[ICP]` mistakes when doing `[job]`”
- “Best `[tool/process]` for `[ICP]`”

# 30‑Day Brand Strategy Plan

## Week 1 — Positioning
- Write the positioning sentence
- Draft the ICP snapshot
- Pick 3 differentiators + proof

## Week 2 — Offer + messaging
- Define offer stack + fast win
- Write homepage hero (headline, subhead, CTA)
- Create a one‑page “mechanism” explanation

## Week 3 — Website conversion
- Implement conversion sequence
- Add lead capture + calendar link
- Add 2 case studies (even small wins)

## Week 4 — Demand engine
- Publish 1 pillar page
- Publish 2 supporting articles
- Repurpose into LinkedIn posts / email
